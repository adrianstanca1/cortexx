Frequently Asked Questions

Does CortexBuild work offline?

Yes — the mobile app caches your active projects locally so you can view timesheets, submit daily reports, and take photos without an internet connection.

Changes made offline are queued and automatically synced to the server the next time your device has connectivity. A sync indicator in the top bar shows you when unsynced data is pending.

Note: creating new projects, inviting team members, and processing payments require an active connection.

How does GPS clock-in work?

When you tap Clock In, the app reads your device's current GPS coordinates and checks whether you are within the geofence radius (default 200 m) set for that job site by your project administrator.

If you are outside the geofence, the clock-in is flagged — your administrator will see a warning on the timesheet. The clock-in is still recorded; the flag is for review purposes only.

Location is accessed only at the moment you clock in or out — we do not track your location continuously. You can review or change the app's location permission in iOS Settings > CortexBuild.

How do I set up or reset Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)?

Go to Account > Security > Two-Factor Authentication in the web app. We support authenticator apps (e.g. Google Authenticator, Authy) via TOTP.

To reset MFA (e.g. if you lost your phone), contact us at support@cortexbuildpro.com from your registered email address. After identity verification we will disable MFA on your account so you can re-enrol.

How does Stripe billing work? Can I cancel at any time?

Subscriptions are billed monthly or annually via Stripe. Your card is charged automatically at the start of each billing period.

You can cancel at any time from Account > Billing. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you retain full access until then. We do not offer partial-period refunds except where required by law.

To update your payment method or download invoices, go to Account > Billing > Manage Subscription, which opens the Stripe customer portal.

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion by emailing admin@cortexbuildpro.com from your registered address with the subject line "Account deletion request".

We will confirm deletion within 5 business days. Your project data, timesheets, and personal information will be permanently removed within 30 days of the request. Database backups containing your data are purged after 90 days.

Note: if you are an organisation admin, you must first transfer or remove all team members before your account can be deleted.

How do I export my data?

Under UK GDPR you have the right to receive a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

To request a data export, email admin@cortexbuildpro.com from your registered address. We will prepare a JSON/CSV export of your account data, project records, timesheets, and daily reports and deliver it within 30 days.

I signed in with Google or Microsoft — can I switch to a password?

Yes. Go to Account > Security > Sign-in Methods and select Add password. Once you set a password you can use either method.

If you want to unlink an OAuth provider, make sure you have a password set first (to avoid being locked out), then remove the provider from the same settings page.

Push notifications stopped working on my iPhone. What should I check?

Try the following steps in order:

  • iOS Settings > CortexBuild > Notifications — ensure "Allow Notifications" is on
  • iOS Settings > Focus / Do Not Disturb — check no mode is suppressing alerts
  • In the app, go to Account > Notifications and toggle your notification preferences off and back on to re-register your device token
  • Sign out of the app fully and sign back in — this forces a fresh token registration

If notifications are still not working after these steps, email us at support@cortexbuildpro.com with your device model and iOS version.
