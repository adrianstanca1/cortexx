Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 April 2026

CortexBuild ("we", "us", "our") operates the CortexBuild web application and iOS mobile application (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights available to you under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

By creating an account or using the Service you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

The data controller for the Service is CortexBuild, reachable at admin@cortexbuildpro.com. Our servers are located on a VPS hosted at cortexbuildpro.com running PostgreSQL and Redis.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

Account data

  • Full name and email address (provided at sign-up or via Google / Microsoft OAuth)
  • OAuth provider user identifiers (Google, Microsoft)
  • Password hash (when using email/password sign-in; never stored in plain text)
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) configuration

Project & work data

  • Project names and site addresses you create within the Service
  • Requests for Information (RFIs), daily reports, and associated free-text notes
  • Site photos and safety-incident photos uploaded by you
  • Voice note audio recordings created for daily reports
  • Digital signatures captured in-app

Location data

  • GPS coordinates used to verify clock-in events within a configurable geofence (default 200 m radius around a job site)
  • Location data attached to safety incident reports
  • We do not track your location continuously; location is only accessed at the moment you initiate a clock-in or file an incident report

Device data

  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) token for routing push notifications to your device
  • Device model and operating-system version (used for push-routing and crash-triage only)

Usage data

  • Page views and feature-interaction events (collected via PostHog analytics, where enabled)
  • Session metadata such as IP address, browser type, and referrer

Billing data

  • Subscription tier and billing status (managed by Stripe)
  • We do not store full card numbers; payment card data is handled exclusively by Stripe and is subject to their PCI-DSS compliance

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and maintain your account and authenticate you securely
  • To deliver the core Service features (project management, timesheets, RFIs, daily reports)
  • To verify clock-in geofencing and process timesheet data
  • To send push notifications for project updates and reminders
  • To process subscription payments and manage billing
  • To analyse product usage and improve the Service
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • To comply with legal obligations

Legal bases (UK GDPR): contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)) for core features; legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) for security and analytics; consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) for optional analytics and marketing communications.

4. Permissions We Request on Your Device

Location (iOS) — requested only when you clock in or file an incident report. We use "While Using the App" permission. We never request "Always On" location access.

Camera & Photo Library — requested when you attach site photos or safety-incident photos. We do not access your photo library without explicit user interaction.

Microphone — requested when you record a voice note for a daily report. Recording stops as soon as you stop the in-app recording session.

Push Notifications — requested to deliver project updates. You may disable this at any time in iOS Settings.

5. Third Parties We Share Data With

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the processors listed below, solely to operate the Service.
  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription management. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Google / Microsoft — OAuth authentication (only when you choose to sign in via these providers). Data shared is limited to what is needed to verify your identity.
  • Apple APNs — delivery of push notifications to iOS devices.
  • PostHog — product analytics (page views and feature events), where enabled. Data is pseudonymised. PostHog Privacy Policy
  • Infrastructure provider — our VPS host processes data on our behalf under a data processing agreement.

We may disclose personal data if required to do so by law or in response to a valid request from a public authority.

6. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in a PostgreSQL database and Redis cache on our VPS at cortexbuildpro.com. Data in transit is protected by TLS 1.2+. Data at rest is protected by server-level disk encryption. We enforce access controls, audit logging, and automated vulnerability scanning on all production systems.

7. Data Retention

  • Project data, timesheets, photos, and associated records are retained for the life of your active subscription plus 30 days after cancellation, to allow for re-activation.
  • Database backups are retained for 90 days, after which they are securely deleted.
  • Account data is deleted within 30 days of an account-deletion request (see Section 8).
  • APNs tokens are deleted when you log out, uninstall the app, or delete your account.
  • Analytics events are retained for up to 12 months.

8. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data portability — request an export of your data in a machine-readable format (JSON/CSV)
  • Restriction — request that we limit processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at admin@cortexbuildpro.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

9. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at admin@cortexbuildpro.com and we will delete it promptly.

10. Cookies & Similar Technologies

The web application uses session cookies required for authentication and security (strictly necessary). We may use analytics cookies (PostHog) where you have consented. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling strictly-necessary cookies will prevent you from signing in.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or via an in-app notice at least 14 days before the change takes effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
