Knowledge base

Where's my data stored?

On your device. CortexBuild Pro is local-first — projects, photos, invoices and notes live in your phone or tablet. We have no copy unless you switch on multi-device sync (Pro tier). Back up regularly via Settings → Database → Export.

How do I install on my iPhone without the App Store?

Open cortexbuildpro.com in Safari. Tap Share → Add to Home Screen. CortexBuild installs as a full-screen, offline-capable app.

Cortex AI gave me a wrong number. What now?

Always cross-check AI output against the real facts — quotes against supplier prices, CIS deductions against HMRC, RAMS against the site survey. Cortex AI is a fast first draft, not a qualified estimator. Email support@cortexbuild.app with what you asked and what it said.

I lost my data after reinstalling.

If multi-device sync was off, we can't recover it — local-device data is under your control. Always run Settings → Database → Export before uninstalling.

Does CortexBuild work with Xero / QuickBooks / Sage?

The Pro tier exports CSV ledgers compatible with all three. A direct API connector is on the roadmap — email us to join the beta.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

In the app: Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription. You keep Pro features until the end of the billing period.

Is CortexBuild CIS-compliant?

CortexBuild calculates CIS deductions for the standard 20% and higher 30% rates and generates a CIS300 monthly return. It's not a replacement for an accountant — have a qualified accountant sign off before filing.

Is CortexBuild GDPR compliant?

Yes. See our Privacy Policy for legal basis, retention, your rights, and how to exercise them.
